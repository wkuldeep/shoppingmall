About Us

Who We Are

Attracting customers to their online sales and marketing venue is a major challenge for shopping mall owners. Consumers have become more used to Internet and mobile technologies, and many prefer the convenience of using retail store software.

Online shopping is a form of electronic commerce which allows consumers to directly buy goods or services from a seller over the Internet using a web browser or a mobile app. Consumers find a product of interest by visiting the website of the retailer directly or by searching among alternative vendors using a shopping search engine, which displays the same product's availability and pricing at different e-retailers. As of 2021, customers can shop online using a range of different computers and devices, including desktop computers, laptops, tablet computers and smartphones.
